Privacy Policy

How we protect and handle your data

Effective Date: December 1, 2024

At CrewChat.ai ("we," "us," or "our"), we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect minimal personal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Basic Profile: First name, age range, and gender (optional)
  • Account Information: Email address for account management
  • Communication Data: Your conversations with AI personas (processed but not permanently stored)

Technical Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Analytics: App performance metrics and crash reports
  • Subscription Data: Purchase information managed through app stores

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Provide AI responses and manage conversations
  • Personalization: Tailor AI personas to your preferences
  • Account Management: Handle subscriptions and customer support
  • App Improvement: Enhance performance and user experience
  • Safety & Security: Detect and prevent misuse

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

Your conversation data is primarily stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption. This ensures your privacy and gives you control over your data.

Cloud Processing

When you send messages to AI personas, your data is:

  • Encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Processed by AI services to generate responses
  • Not permanently stored on our servers
  • Automatically deleted after processing

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers or other third parties.

We may share limited information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help operate our app (e.g., RevenueCat for subscriptions, AI processing APIs)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights and users
  • Business Transfers: In connection with mergers or acquisitions (with prior notice)

5. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and analytics
  • App Store/Google Play: Payment processing
  • AI Processing APIs: Generate AI responses (data not retained)
  • Analytics Services: App performance monitoring

6. Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

  • Access: View your stored data through app settings
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Export: Request copies of your data
  • Correction: Update your profile information

Communication Preferences

  • Opt out of promotional communications
  • Control push notifications in app settings
  • Manage data collection preferences

7. Age Restrictions

CrewChat.ai is intended for users who are 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we discover that we have collected information from a child under 18, we will delete that information immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. When you delete your account:

  • Local conversation data is deleted from your device
  • Account information is removed from our systems
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending you a notification
  • Requiring your acceptance of the updated terms

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@3motionlabs.com
  • Website: www.crewchat.ai

For data protection inquiries in the European Union, you may also contact your local data protection authority.
